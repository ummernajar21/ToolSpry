Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains what data is (and is not) collected when you use SQL Practice.

Overview

SQL Practice is a static, browser-based learning tool. You can use the site without creating an account or providing personal information.

Information we do not collect

  • No names or usernames
  • No email addresses
  • No passwords
  • No SQL queries are stored on our servers

All SQL queries run entirely in your browser.

Cookies and local storage

SQL Practice itself does not use cookies or local storage to track users.

However, third-party services such as advertising networks may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own privacy policies.

Advertising

This site may display advertisements to support ongoing development.

Advertising partners may use cookies or device identifiers to show relevant ads. We do not control how third-party advertisers collect or use data.

You can usually manage ad preferences through your browser or device settings.

Donations

If you choose to support this project through platforms like Ko-fi, payments are handled entirely by the third-party provider.

We do not receive or store your payment information.

External links

This site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via the email address listed on the Contact page.
