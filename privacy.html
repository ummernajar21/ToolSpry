Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

üìå Quick Summary: We respect your privacy. We don't sell your data. We use cookies, analytics, and third-party services (like ads) to keep ToolSpry running. You're in control of your data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to ToolSpry ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at toolspry.tech (the "Site").

By using ToolSpry, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Email address (when you contact us)
  • Name (when you contact us)
  • Any information you include in messages sent to us

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit ToolSpry, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, queries run
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system
  • IP Address: Your approximate location based on IP address
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device (see Section 4)

2.3 Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your preferences (like dark mode settings and challenge progress). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve ToolSpry's functionality
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Monitor and prevent technical issues
  • Display personalized ads (see Section 5)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Site to function (e.g., saving your theme preference)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the Site
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by our ad network to show relevant ads

Cookies Used by ToolSpry:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
theme Essential Saves your dark/light mode preference Permanent
_ga Analytics Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_ga_* Analytics Google Analytics - persists session state 2 years
_gid Analytics Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours

Note: When we add advertising networks (AdSense, Propeller Ads), additional cookies will be used. This policy will be updated accordingly.

Managing Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect Site functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

ToolSpry uses third-party services to operate and monetize the Site. These services may collect and process your data according to their own privacy policies.

5.1 Advertising Network

We use a third-party advertising network to display ads on ToolSpry. These ads help us keep ToolSpry free for everyone.

The ad network may use cookies and tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activity to show personalized ads. This may include:

  • Your browsing behavior on ToolSpry and other websites
  • Device information and IP address
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, views)

Important: We do not control the ad network's data practices. Please review their privacy policy for more information about how they handle your data.

5.2 Ko-fi (Donations)

If you choose to support ToolSpry through Ko-fi, you will be redirected to Ko-fi's platform. Ko-fi processes your payment information and personal data according to their own privacy policy.

Ko-fi's Privacy Policy: https://ko-fi.com/privacy

We do not receive or store your payment information. Ko-fi may share limited information with us (such as your username or donation amount) but not your financial details.

5.3 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how users interact with ToolSpry. Google Analytics collects anonymous data such as:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Your location (country, city - approximate based on IP)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system, screen size)
  • Traffic source (how you found ToolSpry - Google, social media, direct)
  • User behavior (clicks, scrolling, navigation patterns)

Google Analytics Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

How Google Uses Data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect this information. The data helps us:

  • Understand which tutorials are most helpful
  • Identify technical issues or errors
  • Improve content based on user needs
  • Optimize website performance

Your IP address is anonymized by Google Analytics. We cannot identify you personally from this data.

Opt-Out Options:

  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser settings (we respect DNT signals)
  • Use privacy-focused browsers (Brave, Firefox with tracking protection)
  • Block cookies in your browser settings

5.4 Email Service Provider

When you contact us via email, your message is processed through our email service provider. They handle email delivery and storage according to their privacy policy.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share your information:

  • With Service Providers: Third parties that help us operate ToolSpry (e.g., hosting, analytics, ads)
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share your information

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Local storage data (like theme preferences) remains on your device until you clear it.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of certain data collection (e.g., analytics, personalized ads)
  • Cookie Control: Manage cookies through your browser settings

Analytics Opt-Out

To opt-out of Google Analytics tracking:

  • Browser Add-on: Install Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on
  • Do Not Track: Enable in browser settings (we respect DNT signals)
  • Cookie Management: Clear or block analytics cookies in your browser

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@toolspry.tech.

10. Children's Privacy

ToolSpry is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

11. International Users

ToolSpry is hosted and operated in India. If you access the Site from outside this region, your information may be transferred to and processed in India. By using ToolSpry, you consent to this transfer.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of ToolSpry after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: info@toolspry.tech
  • Contact Page: toolspry.tech/contact.html
